About us

Sakuragaoka High School Barbershop Music Club!! (桜高バー音部!! or SKB for short) is the world’s best anime song Barbershop cover group (until someone else makes another one).

Formed in 2024 by a group of Barbershop/a cappella enthusiasts brought together by a shared love for J-music and J-culture, they combine the smooth resonant harmonies of Barbershop music with the energetic youth of (mostly) Anime tunes, to bring a unique crossover style of music to both J-culture and Barbershop music events in Singapore.
